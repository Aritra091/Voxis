About Voxis

Voxis is your all-in-one real-time multilingual AI assistant that sees, hears, and speaks. Designed with the power of Groq and advanced multimodal intelligence, it understands speech, processes images, and reads text—instantly. Whether you're collaborating across borders, using voice commands, or scanning visual content, Voxis bridges communication gaps with smooth, futuristic precision.

Built for developers, creators, educators, and everyday users, Voxis is your gateway to intelligent global interaction.
